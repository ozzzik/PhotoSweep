Privacy Policy – PhotoSweep

Last updated: March 27, 2026.

This policy describes how the PhotoSweep iOS app (“the App”) handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • Photo library analysis runs on your device (Apple Photos + on-device Vision). We do not upload your full photo library to our servers for scanning.
  • Deleting photos is your choice; the App uses system APIs to move items you confirm to Recently Deleted.
  • Advertising may be shown via Google AdMob and/or Unity Ads. Those providers may collect identifiers and usage data under their own policies.
  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card details.

Information we access on device

With your permission, the App accesses your photo library to read image/video metadata and image content for features you run (e.g. duplicate detection, similar grouping, screenshots, large videos). Processing is designed to occur on-device unless a third-party SDK (below) transmits data as part of ads or analytics.

Third-party services

The App may include:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – may use device/advertising identifiers and diagnostics. See Google’s privacy documentation.
  • Unity Ads (if enabled in your build) – may collect device and interaction data under Unity’s policies.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit – subscription status and purchases are handled by Apple.

Review each provider’s privacy policy for details beyond this summary.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the age required in your region). Do not use the App if you are not allowed to agree to these terms in your jurisdiction.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, in-app or App Store notes.
