About the app

PhotoSweep helps you clean up your iPhone or iPad photo library by finding duplicate and similar photos, suggesting a best shot in each group, and helping you remove clutter you choose to delete. Analysis runs on your device; you control what gets moved to Recently Deleted.

The app is free to use with optional advertising. PhotoSweep Premium is an auto-renewing subscription that unlocks additional scan options and removes ads, as described in the app.
